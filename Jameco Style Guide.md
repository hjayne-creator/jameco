**Jameco Electronics**   
**Product Detail Page Content Style Guide**

**Purpose**

Jameco Electronics is a manufacturer and distributor of electronic components. Accuracy is its highest priority. The site should elicit customer trust. Every Jameco product detail page (PDP) should help a buyer confidently answer five questions:

* Is this the exact product I need?  
* Will it work in my application?  
* Is it compatible with my setup?  
* Can I trust this source?  
* Should I buy it now?

PDPs should also be optimized for traditional search engine optimization (SEO), generative engine optimization (GEO) with AI chatbots, and use best practice conversion rate optimization (CRO) techniques.

**Brand Voice**

Use language that is accurate, helpful, professional, clear, technical when needed, concise, trustworthy, consistent, and neutral.

Avoid exaggeration, unsupported claims, vague superlatives, empty marketing language, and ambiguous terminology.

**Core PDP Structure**

Required above the fold:

* Optimized H1 title  
* Product identity block  
* Pricing/stock visibility  
* Trust indicators  
* Key specifications

**H1 Standards**

Must include Brand, Manufacturer Part Number, Product Type, and Core Differentiators.

Example: MEAN WELL LRS-150-12 150W 12V Enclosed Single Output Power Supply

**Product Identity Block**

Required fields:

* Jameco Part Number  
* Manufacturer  
* Manufacturer Part Number  
* Availability  
* Pricing tiers  
* Compliance icons when verified

**Overview Section**

**H2**: This is an alternate search header. It’s designed to be consistent with the product title, but it should also be unique compared to the H1 so that it captures people who search for the product slightly differently.

**Overview**: This is a single paragraph that describes the most important aspects of the product and blends features, benefits, applications & specifications into simple sentences.

**Features**: This section should be bulleted and ordered in terms of priority. It should include features plus benefits. Example: Built-in overload and short-circuit protection helps safeguard connected equipment.

**Applications**: Include only verified or highly logical use cases. Examples: industrial automation, LED systems, robotics, test benches, DIY electronics, PCB housing, prototypes.

**FAQs**: Use 3–5 concise FAQs that remove friction and capture search intent. It includes a brief question and the corresponding answer. Question examples might include: Can it run continuously? Is hardware included? Is it outdoor rated?

**Concerns**: This section should be unique and distinct from the FAQ section and include common customer concerns in a practical and trustworthy tone. There should be a brief response consistent with how a sales engineer might overcome the objection.

**Jameco Site Benefits**. Include any site-wide benefits that may not be included on the product page that are directly relevant to the purchase of this product in a new section called Jameco Site Benefits.

**Specifications Section**

The specifications must be exact, structured, searchable, and standardized. The Specifications section includes parametric data organized as a two-column table with the first column listing the specification name and the second column listing the specification value.

Products in similar categories should present the specifications in a similar order when appropriate.

Use manufacturer terminology when possible.

**SEO Standards**

Use natural buyer phrasing in H1, H2, and all text sections. Include common synonyms, abbreviations, dimensions, and use-case phrases where relevant.

**GEO Standards**

AI systems reward complete, direct answers. Each PDP should clearly state:

* What it is  
* What it does  
* Who uses it  
* Key dimensions/specs  
* Compatibility  
* Certifications  
* Common applications  
* Included accessories when relevant

**CRO Standards**

Reduce hesitation with trust signals:

* Datasheets  
* Compliance documents  
* Real dimensions  
* Quantity pricing  
* Warranty/returns  
* Installation guidance

**Formatting Rules**

Use sentence case, bullets, short paragraphs, and consistent units. Maintain consistent capitalization across similar fields and headings.

Preferred editorial style: inches first, mm second, where applicable. 

Always list content in the order presented in this document. Do not include pricing or stocking levels in this output. Do not include any related products.

Provide both an HTML version that is appropriate to copy and paste as well as a WYSIWYG version.

**Compliance Rules**

Never invent certifications, ratings, temperature ranges, waterproof claims, or warranty terms. If unknown, omit. Do not imply compatibility unless confirmed or highly evident from the product type.

**QA Checklist**

All content must meet the following Q/A standards:

* Correct brand / MPN  
* Optimized H1/H2  
* Accurate specs  
* Useful FAQs  
* No duplicate content  
* Consistent with the existing Jameco PDP language  
* Strong decision-making information
